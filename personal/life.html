My Story

I started off my co-op journey working with Scotiabank's Continuous Integration and Delivery team optimizing internal tools through python scripts. After 1 month, I was noticed by Pavel Volgarev, a product engineer for International Banking for my learning capabilites and interests in software development. I was transferred to work on the development of an internal application called

Prism

. Through Prism, I was able to design, develop and test the application alongside a team of 4 people. This project allowed me to gain insight on various aspects of product development. What I learned was that I loved empathising with user needs and leveraging technology to solve problems.


Ever since Prism, I have come to realize that I have a passion for using technology to impact peoples lives. Product management fit perfectly with what I loved doing. However, what I learned was that in order to be a successful product manager I needed to enhance my software development skills. This is important to me because in order to translate stakeholder requirements into functional designs and specifications, I needed to understand engineering and design. That is what lead me to my last two co-ops at

tealbook

and

FactoryU R&D

.
